message from desk of chairman

Greetings to all parents, students, and supporters,
I am honored to welcome you to Radiant Secondary School, where you will benefit from our competent management, experienced teachers, friendly staff, and positive learning environment. Since our establishment in 2057 B.S. (2000 A.D.), Radiant has consistently produced successful students with an average of 500 graduates each year across various programs. Our well-equipped laboratories and state-of-the-art facilities contribute to a superior academic experience. Radiant meets the nation's need for a school with proper facilities and top-quality education that aligns with international standards. Join us and become a part of the Radiant family.
Sincerely,
The Chairman.
